# Requirements Brief

## Project Goal
Build a polished **5-page consulting website** that helps a professional services business establish credibility, explain its offerings, and convert visitors into inquiries. The site should feel premium, trustworthy, and easy to navigate while remaining fully static.

## Target Pages
1. **Home** – Clear hero message, value proposition, service highlights, trust indicators, and CTA.
2. **About** – Company story, mission, expertise, and team or founder overview.
3. **Services** – Breakdown of offerings with benefits and who each service is for.
4. **Pricing** – Simple package/tier presentation or engagement model explanation.
5. **Contact** – Contact details, inquiry form UI, and call-to-action for booking or emailing.

## Functional Requirements
- Responsive navigation across all pages.
- Reusable header and footer for consistency.
- Clean section hierarchy and strong visual emphasis on CTAs.
- Contact form UI should be present, even if submission is handled manually or via placeholder behavior.
- Use localStorage only if needed for lightweight UI state; no backend required.

## Design Requirements
- Professional consulting aesthetic: minimal, modern, and high-trust.
- Use whitespace, strong typography, and a restrained color palette.
- Include placeholder visuals or abstract graphics rather than requiring external media.
- Mobile-first layout with readable content and accessible spacing.

## MVP Success Criteria
- Visitors can understand the business, offerings, and pricing within a few scrolls.
- The site looks credible on desktop and mobile.
- The contact page makes it obvious how to get in touch.

## Non-Goals
- Authentication, dashboards, real-time features, or complex CMS functionality.
- Backend integrations beyond optional future form handling.
